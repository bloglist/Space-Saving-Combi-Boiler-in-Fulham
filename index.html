Space-Saving Combi Boiler in Fulham

From Old School to High-Efficiency: Transforming a Conventional Heating System to a Space-Saving Combi Boiler in Fulham

Elevating Comfort, Maximizing Space When our first-time customer in Fulham reached out to us, they were looking for more than just a boiler replacement; they were seeking to modernize their home’s heating system. The goal was twofold: enhance heating efficiency and reclaim valuable space by eliminating their hot water cylinder. We turned their dream into reality with our specialized skills and customer-centric approach.

Here’s How We Achieved It:
  • Initial Consultation: We kicked off the project by offering an in-depth consultation, educating the customer on their options and helping them make an informed choice. They were especially impressed with our expertise.
  • Product Choice: A Vaillant ecofit 30kw boiler was selected for its high-efficiency, quality, and extended 10-year warranty.
  • The Powerflush Factor: To ensure the boiler’s longevity and optimal performance, we used a Kamco CF40 machine to powerflush the entire heating system.
  • Quality Testing: Post-installation, we performed water quality tests. Upon discovering inefficiencies, we performed another powerflush to maximize the system’s efficiency.
  • Logistical Challenges: Despite the difficulty with parking and moving equipment, our team managed to efficiently install the system without compromising on quality.
  • Customer Satisfaction: At the end of the day, the customer was not only pleased with the high-level performance of their new system but also with our exceptional service. They were kind enough to leave us a review and have opted for our yearly servicing to maintain their 10-year warranty.
